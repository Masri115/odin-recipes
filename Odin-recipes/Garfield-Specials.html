Garfield's Specials

Description

Discover this rich and Cheeeeezy Lasagna recipe and make all you friends'cat jalous of yours!

Ingredients

  • 1lbs of Cheese
  • 1/2 lbs of beef
  • 1 supermarket can of Tomato Sauce
  • 8 Lasagna Sheet

Steps

  1. Put the meat into a pre hited pan
  2. When cooked add the tomato sauce and let it cook for 10minutes
  3. Put a first layer of sauce in a plate made for the lasagna then add the lasagna sheet and a good amount of cheese. Repeat this Process 3 times
    4. Put it in the oven for 36 minutes. When ready let it rest for 5 minutes and "Bon Appetit"!
